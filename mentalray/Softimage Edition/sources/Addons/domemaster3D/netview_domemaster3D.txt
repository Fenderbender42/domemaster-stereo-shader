Domemaster3D Shader for Softimage - Version 1.6
This edition was released on January 20, 2014.

A 2D/3D angular fisheye and latlong rendering system for use in planetariums, fulldome theatres, and VR headsets. This shader is also available for 3DS Max, and Maya.
